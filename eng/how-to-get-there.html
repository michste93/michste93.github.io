BY CAR:

From the exit Egna-Ora of the Highway A22 of Brennero, take the state road n.48 direction Cavalese. Then go to Tesero and take the S.P. 215 until Stava.

BY TRAIN:

Nearest stations are: Ora, Trento and Bolzan. Then connections by bus.

BY BUS:

Daily direct connections from Trento, Bolzano, Ora to Tesero. Then from Tesero to Stava.

BY PLANE:

The nearest airport is the one of Verona, 150km far away.
