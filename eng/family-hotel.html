FAMILY HOTEL

The Berghotel Miramonti promises to help young and adults to live intense emotions in the nature!

Sport

There is a valley dedicated to children, but especially to those parents who want to surprise and make them laugh trying new experiences and new sports.
​When nature wears its white dress, funny couriers stay with your kids and go walking in the wood to discover the signs of roe deer, or go skiing with ski instructors on ski slopes and tracks or give life to games and stories full of fantasy and creativity at the ski-kindergarten.
During the summer, to move freely in nature, they explore the forests riding a mountain bike, the trees' branches while tarzaning and rocky places when they climb. Across the valley, they can have a fantastic ride along the cycle route or try the rafting adventure on a inflatable down the main water stream of Fiemme.

Fun

All children spend extraordinary days in Val di Fiemme, with dragonists, friends of pixies, lumberjacks, bee-keepers, herbalists, geologists. Between two Natural parks (Paneveggio Pale di San Martino and Monte Corno), nature becomes a surprising stage and gives life to “Spectacular tales”. In the meadows around the villages and at the mountain huts at high altitude, fantastic amusement parks let children explore nature while they are playing. In the acroparks they get more balance and handiness, climbing like monkeys from one branch to the other.
